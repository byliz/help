Resources

Immediate Help

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline phone number: 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • National Suicide Prevention Chat: https://suicidepreventionlifeline.org/chat/
  • Contact USA Crisis Chat: CrisisChat.org
  • Crisis Text Line: text "SAFE" to 741-741
  • USA Emergency police, ambulance, or firefighter phone number: 9-1-1
  • Victim Connect Helpline phone number: 855-4-VICTIM (855-484-2846)
  • The United States Department of Justice. Report Computer, Internet-Related, or Intellectual Property Crime: https://www.justice.gov/criminal-ccips/reporting-computer-internet-related-or-intellectual-property-crime

Online Safety Tools

  • Wireshark: https://www.wireshark.org/, to monitor home network traffic
  • GetLinkInfo.com: http://getlinkinfo.com/, to check suspicious links
  • Google's Web Search Troubleshooter: https://support.google.com/websearch/troubleshooter/3111061?hl=en, to remove personal information like nude photos and credit card information from Google search results
  • Google's Outdated Content Remover: https://www.google.com/webmasters/tools/removals, to remove deleted and altered pages with outdated cache information from Google search and image results
